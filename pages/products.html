ERamen Menu's!

Shoyu Ramen

Soy sauce-based broth with chicken or pork broth and toppings of chashu, menma, and scallions.

Miso Ramen

Hearty broth made from fermented soybean, combined with chicken broth and topped with sliced pork, and corn.

Tonkotsu Ramen

Creamy, milky broth made from boiled pork bones and served with chashu, boiled egg, and green onions.

Spicy Ramen

Broth made with spicy chili paste such as gochujang, sriracha, and topped with sliced pork, and bamboo shoots.

Vegetarian Ramen

Vegetable-based broth with tofu, corn, and assorted fresh vegetables as toppings.

Tsukemen Ramen

Dipping-style ramen with thicker and firmer noodles served separately from a concentrated and flavorful broth
