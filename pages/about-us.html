About Us

ERamen shop is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. It was established since 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia since 2015. All ERamen menu made by organic flour and turn into delicious ramen noodle. Customer can find any kind of ramen, such as ramen and so many kind of tempura.

ERamen shop envisions becoming the top choice for ramen lovers seeking an authentic and traditional dining experience. They pride themselves on using organic ingredients, especially in their homemade ramen noodles, to create a perfect balance of texture and flavor. Their goal is to transport customers to the vibrant streets of Japan through their culinary expertise, fostering a love for ramen that transcends borders.

ERamen shop is dedicated to providing an exceptional ramen experience that combines culinary expertise, warm hospitality, and a commitment to quality. They aim to preserve the essence of traditional ramen recipes while incorporating innovative flavors and techniques. Sustainability, supporting local farmers, and caring for their employees are integral parts of their mission. Ultimately, they strive to create lasting memories and spread joy through their delicious ramen dishes.
